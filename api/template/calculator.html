Shastadari Units of Measure ‚öô¬†

Civil time is divided into six units; one day is divided regularly into sixths, and we group those sixths into units as such:

  • the uta, symbol uta, corresponding to the first two divisions, closer to the hour;
  • the posha, symbol pox, corresponding to the next two divisions, closer to the minute;
  • the agrima, symbol agm, corresponding to the next two divisions, closer to the second;
  • the anuga, symbol ang, corresponding to the next two divisions, closer to the centisecond;
  • the boda, symbol bod, corresponding to the last two divisions, closer to the millisecond;

So, one day is divided:
